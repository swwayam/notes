
*********** Series ************

Series: is one-dimensional labeled array capable of holding any data type. This axis labels are collectively referred to as the index.

Attributes: Attributes return the information about the object.

Methods: Perform actions and can even change the original object.


********** DataFrame **********

DataFrame: It is a 2 dimensional labeled data structure with columns of potentially different types. You can think of it like a spreadsheet or SQL table, or a dict of Series Object. It is generally the most commonly used pandas object.

- Essentially is just comprised of 2 or more series
- axis = 0 refers to the index.
- axis = 1 refers to the columns.
